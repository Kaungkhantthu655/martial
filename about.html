Our Story

Since 2005

DoBu Martial Arts was founded with a vision to create a community where traditional martial arts meet modern training techniques. Our name "DoBu" combines the Korean word for "way" (Do) with the Japanese word for "martial" (Bu), representing our diverse approach to martial arts.
